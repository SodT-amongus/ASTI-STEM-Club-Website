STEM Club

Our STEM Club is a place for curious minds to explore science, technology, engineering, and math through hands-on projects and real-world challenges. Members collaborate to build, code, experiment, and problem-solve while developing creativity, teamwork, and critical thinking skills. Whether you're a beginner or a future engineer, the STEM Club is a fun and supportive environment to learn and innovate.
